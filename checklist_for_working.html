Residence and Access to the Labor Market

Before recognition of asylum (asylum seekers), work permit is needed to work in Belgium, the procedure regarding work permits has been changed since 3 January 2019. From now on, for a labor migrant's employment for more than 90 days, a combined permit must be applied for. This is an electronic residence card that contains both an admission to work and an admission to stay.
In addition, the work permit C is abolished. Those who used to qualify for a work permit C can now work by law on the basis of their temporary residence permit. You can find detail regarding work permit at the websites of the Agency for inclusion and integration and werk.be.

Can't find the answer to your question? Contact the legal help desk of the Integration and Integration Agency.




Recognition of Your Diploma

Recognition of your diploma is a plus for you to find a job, however it is not a must. For more information, visit the Diploma Recognition page.

Important tips and facts

You can also have your experiences and competences recognized

You can have them tested by a recognized EVC organization.

You will then receive an EVC certificate.

You can use this certificate for exemptions in training or on the labor market.




Professional Card

If you want to work in Belgium as a self-employed person, you must apply for a professional card as a foreigner. This is the permission of the Belgian government to pursue an independent professional activity in Belgium. You can find the detail at here.

Where you can apply for a professional card, search and find them here.




Good Knowledge of Dutch

Even though your job would be English based, many companies would like to hire employees who speaks Dutch, so that learning Dutch, at least level B1 is a kind of must to easily find a job in Flemish Labor market.
For more information, visit the Language Learning page.





Enough IT Knowledge

The world is in the stage of Industrial Revaluation 4.0, It means that the requirement for basic IT knowledge is a must, not all jobs require the same amount of IT knowledge, however basic skills is required in almost every kind of jobs. For example, you will almost always come into contact with programs such as Word and Excel. Test your knowledge of these programs. If you want to test your general IT knowledge, you can take the Digital Literacy test.


Important tips and facts: VDAB offers job seekers a digital starter package to facilitate the job search.
